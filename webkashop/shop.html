about company

Suleiman Demirel University was opened in 1996 with the participation of the First President of Kazakhstan – Elbasy Nursultan Nazarbayev and the 9th President of Turkey – Suleiman Demirel. The main goal of the Strategic Development Plan of the University is to become one of the best international universities in the country and throughout Central Asia. SDU began its educational activity with a small building in the Orbita microdistrict in Almaty, then the university settled in Kaskelen, where a modern campus with an area of 40,000 sq.m and a dormitory with an area of 20,000 sq.m meeting all modern requirements was founded. To date, SDU has a staff of 271 professors, and more than 7000 students are studying.
